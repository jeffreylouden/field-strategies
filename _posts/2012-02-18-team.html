Field Strategies’ leadership, backed by a nationwide team of operatives, brings decades of experience and an unyielding commitment to winning.

  • Matt Schneider

    Co-Founder and Managing Partner

    Matt has been recruited by key decision makers to plan and execute critical programs for the Democratic Party and their progressive allies. His ingenuity and tireless work ethic have put Field Strategies at the forefront of nearly every major national political and issue campaign in last 12 years.

  • Ben Tevelin

    Partner

    For the past 15 years, Ben Tevelin’s commitment to innovative voter contact strategies has guided his work for presidential, gubernatorial, congressional and municipal candidates. Ben also leads much of Field Strategies’ work designing custom programs for national labor unions including AFSCME, SEIU and UAW.

  • Jennifer Holcomb

    Project Manager

    Since joining in 2010, Jennifer Holcomb has managed winning efforts up and down the ballot across the country. In 2012, PolitickerNJ singled out Jennifer’s field effort for Bill Pascrell, Jr. as the determining factor in the race ranked as the Top Contest of the Last Five Years.

  • Daniel Carles

    Project Manager

    Daniel Carles has helped plan and lead over 50 campaigns and GOTV operations since 2002. A New Jersey native, Daniel co-led FS’ efforts in 2012 in support of Congressman Bill Pascrell, Jr, an upset victory hailed by the press as “one of the all-time beatdowns”.

  • Ronnie West

    Project Manager
    Co-Leader FS West

    Ronnie West co-manages Field Strategies’ West Coast operations. In addition to his experience managing high-profile legislative and municipal elections in Southern and Northern California, Ronnie also led much of FS’ massive voter registration efforts in 2012 battleground states.

  • Ryan Garner

    Project Manager
    Co-Leader FS West

    Ryan Garner co-manages Field Strategies’ West Coast operations. Ryan specializes in independent expenditure efforts and grassroots legislative campaigns and has built winning efforts for labor groups, state parties and candidates.

  • Kerissa Barron

    Political Assistant

    Kerissa Barron helps coordinate campaign plans, logistics, and scheduling for FS. Kerissa’s prior political experience includes Gubernatorial and municipal campaigns in her native California, as well as a successful ballot initiative campaign in Maryland.
